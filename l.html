Leaders who fought for Independence

Turar Ryskulov

(December 26, 1894 - February 8, 1938)

Leader of the national liberation movement of the Turkic peoples of Turkestan, outstanding statesman and public figure, prominent scientist, publicist, historian, diplomat.

Alikhan Bukeikhanov

March 5, 1866 - September 27, 1937

Kazakh statesman, political and public figure, teacher, journalist and ethnographer. Founder and one of the leaders of the Alash party.

Akhmet Baitursynov

September 1872 - December 8, 1937

Kazakh educator, linguist, literary critic, Turkologist, poet and translator, public figure and political publicist. Member of the Alash party.

Sultanbek Kozhanova

September 10, 1894 - Death February 10, 1938

Kazakh Soviet socio-political and statesman, scientist, publicist, editor.

Saken Seifullin

October 15, 1894 - April 25, 1938

Kazakh poet and writer, founder of modern Kazakh literature, statesman, prominent member of the Bolshevik Communist Party. Founder of the Writers' Union of Kazakhstan.

Mirzhakip Dulatuly

November 25, 1885 - October 5, 1935

Outstanding Kazakh educator, public figure, poet, writer, ardent speaker, one of the liberal democratic intellectuals.
